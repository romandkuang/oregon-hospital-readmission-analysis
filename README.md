# Oregon Hospital Readmission Analysis (FY 2026)

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes Oregon hospital performance using the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) Hospital Readmissions Reduction Program (HRRP) dataset for FY 2026.

The objective was to identify hospitals, counties, and clinical conditions associated with elevated readmission risk and to evaluate whether hospital quality ratings are associated with readmission performance.

Key findings indicate that Oregon hospitals generally performed better than the CMS benchmark, although several facilities exceeded expected readmission levels and may warrant additional quality improvement review.
---

## Business Problem

Hospital readmissions are a major quality and financial concern for healthcare organizations.

Under the CMS Hospital Readmissions Reduction Program (HRRP), hospitals with excess readmissions may face reductions in Medicare reimbursement.

Healthcare leaders need to identify:

- Which hospitals exceed expected readmission benchmarks
- Which clinical conditions contribute most to readmission risk
- Whether quality ratings are associated with readmission performance
- Geographic areas that may require intervention
